About Us

Somali Jobs Portal was created to connect job seekers with employers across Somalia. Our mission is to empower young professionals by providing them with equal access to employment opportunities.

Help

  • How do I apply? Visit the "Apply" page and fill out the form with your details and resume.
  • Can I post a job? Yes! Contact us via our "Contact" page for more information.
  • Is this service free? Yes, both job seekers and employers can use the platform at no cost.
